Who We Are

We are a team of three undergraduate students working together on this CHE110 project to spread awareness about ecosystems, their functions, and the growing environmental challenges of our world. Our goal is to present clear, engaging, and well-researched information to help students and readers understand how ecosystems work and how we can protect them.

Our Mission

The aim of our project is to educate people about the importance of ecosystems, highlight their services, identify threats, and promote sustainable practices. Through this website, we hope to encourage responsible behavior and inspire others to take small yet meaningful steps toward environmental conservation.

Why We Created This Website

With increasing pollution, climate change, species extinction, and resource depletion, understanding ecosystems is more important than ever. Through this project, we aim to:

  • Explain ecosystem components and their interactions
  • Highlight the benefits and services ecosystems provide
  • Discuss major hazards and threats
  • Promote preventive and sustainable practices

We hope this website helps you learn something new and encourages you to think more deeply about our environment.
